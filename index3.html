about me

I've always sought out opportunities and challenges that are meaningful to me. Although my professional path has taken many twists and turns — from creating online seminars for bariatric patients, to building Georgia Tech’s Online Masters of Computer Science Degree program, to completely switching careers from video production to coding — I've never stopped engaging my passion to help others and solve problems.

As a web developer, I enjoy using my obsessive attention to detail, my unequivocal love for making things, and my mission-driven work ethic to literally change the world. That's why I’m excited to make a big impact at a high growth company.
